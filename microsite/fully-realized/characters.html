Characters

Shinji Ikari

AGE: 14

HEIGHT: 157 cm

ROLE: Pilot of Evangelion Unit-01

The protagonist of the series, a shy and introverted teenage boy who becomes the pilot of the Evangelion Unit-01. He is thrust into the conflict against the Angels and grapples with his sense of self-worth and desire for acceptance, particularly from his estranged father, Gendo Ikari.

Misato Katsuragi

AGE: 29

HEIGHT: 170 cm

ROLE: Operations Director at NERV

The operations director at NERV, the organization responsible for the Evangelions. She is responsible for the strategic deployment of the pilots. Misato is carefree and messy in her personal life but is a capable and serious leader. She has a complicated past.

Asuka Soryu

AGE: 14

HEIGHT: 162 cm

ROLE: Pilot of Evangelion Unit-02

A confident and often abrasive girl who pilots the Evangelion Unit-02. She is of German and Japanese descent and prides herself on her independence and piloting skills. Asuka struggles with deep-seated issues of abandonment and a need for validation.
